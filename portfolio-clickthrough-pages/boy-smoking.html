Boy-smoking

JLW has been filling 9"x6" hardback sketchbooks with drawings since 2010. Mainly line drawings in black and white only, JLW crafts an expressionistic style. Making use of single-line and hatching techniques as well as a simple quiver of micron and fabre castell pens, JLW aims to execute a childlike yet strangely sophisticated resemblance of various subjects. This drawing was inspired by a photograph by 1930's era photographer Dorothea Lange.
